About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master’s Degree in computer science and technology at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Chao Ma.

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor’s Degree in software engineering from South China University of Technology, advised by Prof. Mingkui Tan.

My research mainly aims to build robust and reliable intelligent systems in the field of computer vision and deep learning. I did some research on few-shot learning, domain generalization, and face information security previously. My primary focus recently is on the effective understanding of the 3D world.

Lightning NeRF: Efficient Hybrid Scene Representation for Autonomous Driving

Junyi Cao, Zhichao Li, Naiyan Wang, Chao Ma
In IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2024.
Key points: We propose an efficient hybrid scene representation for autonomous driving scenarios that reduces time complexity for training and rendering via the geometry prior from LiDAR observations.

End-to-End Reconstruction-Classification Learning for Face Forgery Detection

Junyi Cao, Chao Ma, Taiping Yao, Shen Chen, Shouhong Ding, Xiaokang Yang
In IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2022.
Key points: We train a reconstruction network over genuine faces only and use the output of the latent feature by the encoder to perform binary classification.
